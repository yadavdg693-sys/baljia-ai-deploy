# Baljia AI

**Your AI Angel -- runs your company while you enjoy life.**

## The Problem

Building a company takes a team. You need someone to build the product, someone to find customers, someone to run marketing, someone to handle support, someone to manage ads, someone to do outreach. Most founders can't afford that team. So they try to do everything themselves -- and burn out before their idea ever gets a real chance.

The gap isn't ideas. It's execution.

## What Baljia Does

Baljia is an AI platform that runs your company for you. You sign up, tell us your idea, and our AI team takes over -- building, marketing, and growing your business autonomously, 24/7.

You focus on the vision. Baljia handles everything else.

### How It Works

1. **Sign up and tell us what you want to build.**
   You can describe your idea, share your existing business URL, or just say "surprise me" -- we'll research your background and find an opportunity that fits you.

2. **Your company gets set up in minutes.**
   Website, database, email, market research, business strategy, and a roadmap -- all created automatically based on your idea and profile.

3. **AI runs your operations around the clock.**
   Your AI team handles engineering, marketing, customer outreach, social media, advertising, support, analytics, and more. Tasks get planned, executed, and verified without you having to manage anything.

4. **You stay in control.**
   Everything runs through a simple dashboard. Chat with your AI CEO, approve or reject tasks, review progress, and see exactly what's happening at all times. Nothing runs without your visibility.

5. **Your company improves while you sleep.**
   Every night, the platform checks what's working, what's broken, and what's missing -- then acts on it. Bug fixes, new features, marketing improvements, all handled automatically.

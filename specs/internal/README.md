# Internal Specs

These are the full-fidelity subsystem specs.

Use them when:

- turning knowledge into implementation intent
- preserving edge cases and nuance
- resolving what belongs in a subsystem
- deciding what must stay exact vs what may improve

Every internal spec must have:

- founder-visible contract
- hidden-system contract
- acceptance criteria
- implementation-freedom section
- traceability back to topic docs, facts, and decisions

Do not remove source detail from `knowledge/` after writing an internal spec.

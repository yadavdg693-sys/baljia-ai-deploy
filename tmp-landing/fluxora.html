Fluxora
RFQ pipeline software for export sales teams

Stop losing buyers to buried RFQs

Fluxora is a web app for export sales teams handling inbound buyer inquiries across marketplaces, email, and web forms. It keeps every quote, deadline, and follow-up visible before serious opportunities go cold.

01

Capture inbound RFQs

Pull inquiries from Alibaba, email, and web forms into one structured pipeline. Each request becomes a trackable deal instead of another message buried in separate tools.

02

Generate sales documents

Create branded quotations and proforma invoices from saved company and product data. Reuse item details, terms, and business information without retyping them for every buyer.

03

Track follow-up work

Keep deadlines, notes, and next actions attached to each inquiry. The team can see which buyer was quoted, which document was sent, and who needs a follow-up today.

How it works

  1. 1

    Bring inquiries in

    Inbound RFQs from marketplaces, email, and web forms are collected into one place and organized as deals.

  2. 2

    Build the quote

    Use saved company, product, and pricing details to prepare branded quotations and proforma invoices in the required language.

  3. 3

    Manage next steps

    Track deadlines, notes, and follow-up dates so each live inquiry keeps moving instead of stalling between messages.

What makes this different

Unlike spreadsheets, inboxes, and physical files, it treats every RFQ as a deal with status, deadline, notes, and next action.

Unlike marketplace RFQ tools, it combines Alibaba inquiries with direct email and web-form requests in one quotation workflow.

Unlike generic CRM setups, it starts with export quoting work itself: multi-language quotes, proforma invoices, and reusable product data.

Quoting should feel like selling, not cleanup.

Fluxora is being shaped around the real sequence of export work: receive the inquiry, prepare the document, send it, and know exactly who needs attention next. The goal is a repeatable quoting process that does not depend on memory, scattered chats, or one overloaded assistant.

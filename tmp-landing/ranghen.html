RangHen
Calmer appointment follow-up for solo dental clinics

Stop losing chairs to unconfirmed visits

RangHen is a web app for solo dental clinics managing next-day appointments. It helps staff confirm visits early, spot likely no-shows, and start the morning with a steadier schedule.

What it does

Upload next-day schedules

Staff can paste a typed schedule or upload a spreadsheet with tomorrow's appointments. The app turns that list into a reminder queue without manual re-entry on a personal phone.

Send Vietnamese reminders

Automatic SMS messages go out at a set time in Vietnamese. Patients get a clear prompt to confirm, cancel, or ask to move their visit.

Track reply status

Replies appear in one simple dashboard showing confirmed, unanswered, and canceled visits. A risk list highlights appointments that may need a follow-up call before the day begins.

How it works

  1. 1

    Add tomorrow's appointments

    Upload a spreadsheet or type the next day's schedule into the app.

  2. 2

    Send reminder messages

    The app sends scheduled SMS reminders to patients and waits for their replies.

  3. 3

    Review and follow up

    Staff checks the dashboard for confirmations, unanswered visits, and likely no-shows, then calls or reschedules where needed.

What makes this different

  • It focuses on one daily job—confirming tomorrow's appointments—instead of trying to replace an entire practice management system.
  • Compared with manual spreadsheets, phone calls, and ad hoc SMS, it keeps reminders and patient replies in one place with clear status tracking.
  • Unlike heavier systems such as Open Dental or Dentrix, it is aimed at solo clinics that need no-show prevention without extra feature bloat.

Each morning should begin with a clearer schedule and fewer empty chairs.

Built in VN, RangHen is meant to make appointment follow-up a small, reliable routine instead of the task that gets skipped when the clinic is busy. The goal is simple: confirm tomorrow's visits before today ends, while there is still time to adjust the day.

Plinor
Product planning and delivery for teams and agents

Plan products with shared context

Plinor is a product development workspace for modern product teams and AI agents. It keeps planning, execution, and delivery connected so work moves forward without losing the story behind it.

What it does

Turn ideas actionable

Capture product ideas and turn them into issues with the relevant context attached. Specs, discussions, and decisions stay connected to the work instead of being scattered across tools.

Run cycles clearly

Shape cycles, assign work, and track progress in one place. Teams can see what is planned, what is blocked, and what is moving toward release.

Connect work streams

Link Slack, GitHub, and Figma so conversations, code, and designs stay tied to each issue. AI agents can use that shared context to draft tasks, summarize progress, and keep projects moving.

How it works

  1. 1

    Capture the work

    Start with an idea, spec, or request and turn it into trackable work with the right context attached.

  2. 2

    Plan the cycle

    Group issues into cycles, assign owners, and make priorities visible across product, design, engineering, and AI-assisted execution.

  3. 3

    Follow to shipped

    Track progress from discussion to code change so delivery stays visible without constant status chasing.

What makes this different

  • Where Jira can feel heavy for fast-moving teams, Plinor is focused on quick onboarding and low process overhead.
  • Unlike Plane's open-source-led path, Plinor is positioned as a polished commercial workspace built for daily team use.
  • Compared with smaller players like Kitemaker, Plinor is centered on a broader operating model where teams and AI agents share the same execution context.

Product work should read like one continuous story.

Plinor is built around a simple idea: planning, tracking, and building should live together so teams can see decisions turn into shipped work. Built in VN, with a global product workflow in mind.

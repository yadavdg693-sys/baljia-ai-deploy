Rangio
A lighter way to manage appointment recovery

Stop losing patients to missed appointments

Rangio helps solo dental clinics send SMS reminders and handle simple rescheduling from a lightweight web app. It reduces reminder calls and gives staff a faster way to recover empty chair time.

What it does

Daily list upload

Staff upload the day's appointment list into the app instead of tracking reminders on paper or in message threads.

Reply-based confirmations

Patients receive Vietnamese SMS reminders and can confirm with a simple text reply, giving the front desk a clearer view of who is expected.

No-show slot recovery

When someone misses a visit, the system can send a self-serve link showing only the reschedule times the clinic already chose to offer.

How it works

  1. 1

    Upload today's schedule

    The clinic adds its appointment list for the day into the web app.

  2. 2

    Send reminder texts

    Rangio sends SMS reminders and records which patients confirm by reply.

  3. 3

    Offer preset rebooking

    If a patient misses a visit, the app can text a link with the clinic's pre-set reschedule slots.

What makes this different

  • Unlike Open Dental, the focus is not a full practice management system; it stays narrow around reminders, confirmations, and missed-visit recovery.
  • Compared with DoctorConnect, it avoids bundling a broad EHR and AI scribe feature set when a solo clinic may only need a lightweight reminder workflow.
  • Where tools like Weave can center on broader patient communication, Rangio is designed around one operational problem: fewer manual reminder calls and faster rescheduling.

A missed appointment should become a quick recovery task, not a lost hour.

Rangio is being shaped around the day-to-day reality of small clinic teams that need a cleaner schedule and fewer manual follow-ups. Built in VN.

Orderloom
Order coordination for lean export teams

Every order, one dashboard

Orderloom is a web app for export sales coordinators and owner-operators handling cross-border orders. It keeps quotes, samples, documents, and payment follow-ups visible before delays compound.

What it does

Order status hub

Each customer order gets a single view that brings together RFQs, supplier quotes, sample approvals, production milestones, shipping paperwork, and payment follow-ups.

Deadline and blocker tracking

Coordinators can see what is due, what is blocked, and who needs to act next without searching across inboxes, chat threads, and spreadsheets.

Reminder-driven follow-up

Automated reminders flag late samples, missing documents, and overdue payments early, so issues surface before they turn into rushed calls and shipment delays.

How it works

  1. 1

    Open an order

    Create a customer order record and attach the incoming RFQ, quote requests, and key dates to one place.

  2. 2

    Track each milestone

    Update supplier quotes, sample approvals, production progress, shipping documents, and payment status as the order moves forward.

  3. 3

    Resolve what slips

    Use reminders and visible blockers to follow up on late items before they become missed commitments.

What makes this different

  • Unlike spreadsheets plus Zalo and email, it treats the order as the system of record instead of scattering updates across separate tools and memory.
  • Compared with Odoo Online, the focus is narrower: order coordination without the overhead and complexity of a full ERP.
  • Compared with PandaDoc AI, it goes past quoting into sample tracking, production milestones, shipping document checklists, and payment follow-ups.

Export coordination should end the day clear, not reconstructed.

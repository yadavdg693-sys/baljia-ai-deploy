Milesto
Shipment workspace for export coordination teams

Stop running shipments across scattered tools

Milesto is a web workspace for export coordinators and owner-operators handling repeat shipments. It keeps milestones, supplier follow-ups, and export files in one current record.

What it does

  1. 01

    Supplier PO follow-up

    Track each supplier purchase order against the shipment and record who is late, what changed, and what still needs confirmation.

  2. 02

    Shipment milestone timeline

    Update ETD, booking, cargo readiness, and document status on one timeline so the latest shipment picture lives in a single place.

  3. 03

    Document pack assembly

    Generate a shipment pack with the commercial invoice, packing list, booking details, and a ready-to-send customer email from the same record.

How it works

1. Create one shipment. Start a shipment record and attach the suppliers, booking details, dates, and document requirements tied to that export.

2. Update as replies arrive. Capture supplier, forwarder, and buyer updates directly on the shipment so ETD, readiness, and missing files stay visible.

3. Send the full pack. Compile the current shipment documents and customer update into one package instead of rebuilding the story across tabs and chats.

What makes this different

— It replaces spreadsheet, email, and chat handoffs with one shipment record instead of asking teams to reconcile the same update in three places.

— It gives exporters direct operational control without making a freight forwarder the place where shipment truth gets rebuilt.

— It stays focused on export coordination work, rather than the broader setup and customization burden that can come with a full ERP like Odoo.

Export work should feel like a shipment desk you trust.

Built in VN, Milesto is aimed at making every booking change, supplier delay, and document gap visible in one place. When the record stays current, customer updates become part of the workflow instead of extra work at the end of the day.

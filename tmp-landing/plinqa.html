Plinqa
Product work, planned and shipped in one place

Clear product work from request to release

Plinqa is a product development system for product, design, and engineering teams. It keeps requests, priorities, and delivery in one place so work moves without constant manual coordination.

What it does

Capture incoming work

Collect customer feedback, bug reports, and internal requests in one system. Turn loose inputs into trackable items with ownership, status, and priority.

Organize delivery queues

Triage issues, shape backlog items, and group related work into scoped projects. Teams can keep planning close to the work that is actually being built.

Connect execution tools

Link product work to tools like GitHub and Slack so updates stay tied to the source task. That reduces handoff gaps between planning and delivery.

How it works

  1. 1

    Capture a request

    Start with feedback, a bug, or an internal ask and record it as a structured item.

  2. 2

    Triage and scope

    Assign priority, owner, and status, then group related tasks into backlog items or projects.

  3. 3

    Track to completion

    Move work through repeatable workflows while keeping updates connected across the tools your team already uses.

What makes this different

  • Built to avoid the complexity and steep setup burden that often make Jira feel heavy for fast-moving teams.
  • Goes beyond GitHub Issues by giving product and design stakeholders a clearer place to work alongside engineering.
  • Designed as the system where humans and AI operate inside the same workflow, not as separate tools producing disconnected output.

Product teams and AI should work from the same system.

Plinqa is being shaped around a simple idea: planning, decisions, and execution belong in one place. The result should be a calmer way to decide what matters next and get it built.
